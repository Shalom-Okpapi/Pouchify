Getting Started

Setting up your Pouchify shop is quick, easy, and free. Follow these steps to begin selling your artwork:

Create an Artist Account

Sign up for a free Pouchify account. Choose "Artist" when prompted to set up your shop.

Complete Your Profile

Add a profile picture, bio, and links to your social media. A complete profile helps customers connect with you.

Set Up Payment Information

Enter your PayPal or bank account details to receive royalties. This is required before you can upload designs.

Upload Your First Design

Use our simple upload tool to add your artwork. You'll select which products to feature your design on.

Publish and Promote

Once approved, your designs will be available for sale. Share your work on social media to drive traffic.

No Upfront Costs

It's completely free to sell on Pouchify. We only make money when you do - we handle production, shipping, and customer service.

Uploading Designs

Follow these guidelines when preparing and uploading your artwork:

File Requirements

  • Format: PNG, JPG, or GIF
  • Size: At least 2800x2800 pixels
  • DPI: 150+ recommended
  • Color Mode: RGB
  • Max File Size: 30MB

Design Tips

  • Use transparent backgrounds (PNG) for best results
  • Keep important elements within the "safe zone"
  • Consider different product templates when designing
  • Test how your design looks on various products

Upload Process

  1. Click "Add New Work" in your dashboard
  2. Upload your design file
  3. Add a title, description, and tags
  4. Select which products to feature your design on
  5. Set your default royalty percentage (20% recommended)
  6. Submit for review (typically takes 1-2 business days)

Design Approval

All designs are reviewed before going live. Common rejection reasons include:

  • Copyright infringement
  • Low quality/resolution
  • Offensive content
  • Watermarks or logos

Royalties & Payments

You earn a royalty on every product sold with your design. Here's how it works:

Royalty Calculator

Your Earnings: $4.00 per sale

Payment Information

  • Minimum Payout: $20.00
  • Payment Methods: PayPal or bank transfer
  • Payment Schedule: Monthly, around the 15th
  • Tax Forms: May be required for certain earnings thresholds

Royalty Adjustments

Your actual earnings may vary due to:

  • Regional pricing adjustments
  • Currency conversion fees
  • Product-specific base price changes

Best Practices for Success

Maximize your sales with these proven strategies:

Optimize Your Listings

  • Use descriptive, keyword-rich titles
  • Write detailed descriptions with relevant tags
  • Add up to 15 tags per design
  • Use all 5 image slots to show different products

Build Your Portfolio

  • Upload regularly to increase visibility
  • Create collections around themes
  • Offer designs in multiple color variations
  • Create seasonal/holiday-specific artwork

Promote Your Work

  • Share on social media with product links
  • Engage with fans in the Pouchify community
  • Create a portfolio website linking to your shop
  • Run occasional sales or promotions

Analytics Dashboard

Use your artist dashboard to track:

  • Earnings trends over time

Copyright & Intellectual Property

Protect your work and respect others' rights:

Your Rights

  • You retain full copyright to your original work
  • Pouchify only gets license to print/sell products
  • You can remove designs anytime
  • DMCA process available for infringement cases

Prohibited Content

  • No copyrighted material you don't own
  • No trademarks, logos, or brand names
  • No fan art without proper licensing
  • No offensive or illegal content

Fan Art Policy

Fan art is only allowed if:

  • It's sufficiently original/transformative
  • You have proper licensing agreements
  • It doesn't infringe on trademarks (names, logos)

When in doubt, consult a legal professional.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to sell on Pouchify?

It's completely free to sell on Pouchify. There are no upfront costs, monthly fees, or hidden charges. We only make money when you make a sale, taking our cut from the product base price before calculating your royalty.

How long does it take for designs to be approved?

Most designs are reviewed within 1-2 business days. During peak times or for complex designs, it may take up to 5 business days. You'll receive an email notification once your design is approved or if any issues need to be addressed.

Can I sell the same designs on other platforms?

Yes! Pouchify doesn't require exclusivity. You're free to sell your designs on other print-on-demand platforms, your own website, or anywhere else. Many successful artists diversify across multiple platforms.

How do I increase my sales?

To increase sales:

  1. Upload high-quality, original designs regularly
  2. Optimize your titles, descriptions, and tags
  3. Promote your work on social media
  4. Create designs for trending topics/niches
  5. Engage with the Pouchify community
  6. Offer designs on popular products
