Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with an item, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Eligibility

  • Items must be unused and in original condition
  • Original tags and packaging must be intact
  • Proof of purchase required
  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days

Refund Details

  • Refunds issued to original payment method
  • Processing takes 3-5 business days after we receive return
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Return shipping costs are customer's responsibility

Exception for Damaged/Defective Items

If your item arrived damaged or defective, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We'll cover return shipping and send a replacement at no cost.

Exchange Policy

We're happy to exchange items for a different size, color, or style, subject to availability.

Option Exchange Process Timeframe
Size/Color Exchange Return original item and we'll ship replacement Within 30 days
Different Product Exchange Return original item and place new order Within 30 days
International Exchanges Return original item and place new order Within 30 days

Exchange Limitations

Exchanges are subject to product availability. If the item you want is out of stock, we'll issue a refund instead.

How to Start a Return

Follow these simple steps to return an item:

Initiate Your Return

Go to the Returns Center and enter your order number and email address. Select the items you want to return and the reason for return.

Print Return Label

After approval, you'll receive a prepaid return label by email. Print this label and include it with your return package.

Package Your Item

Securely pack the item in its original packaging with all tags attached. Include the completed return form.

Ship Your Return

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location. You'll receive an email when we process your return.

Return Without Original Packaging?

If you no longer have the original packaging, use a sturdy box and sufficient padding. The item must still be in new, unused condition to qualify for a refund.

Return Timeline

Here's what to expect during the return process:

Day 1

You initiate the return through our Returns Center

Day 1-2

We approve your return and email return instructions

Day 3-7

You ship the return package back to us

Day 7-14

We receive and inspect your return

Day 14-17

We process your refund (3-5 business days to show in your account)

Holiday Return Period

Items purchased between November 1 and December 31 may be returned until January 31 of the following year.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Gift cards
  • Used or damaged items (not in original condition)

Wrong Item Received?

If you received an incorrect item, please contact us immediately with photos of the product and packaging. We'll arrange for return shipping and send the correct item.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my refund?

After we receive your return, please allow:

  • Credit card refunds: 3-5 business days to process, plus 1-2 billing cycles to appear on your statement
  • PayPal refunds: 3-5 business days
  • Gift card refunds: Immediate upon processing

You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed.

What if I lost my return label?

If you've misplaced your return label:

  1. Check your email (including spam folder) for the original label
  2. Log in to the Returns Center to reprint your label
  3. If you're still having trouble, contact support with your return ID

Can I return an item I bought on sale?

Yes, sale items can be returned within the standard 30-day window unless marked as "Final Sale." However:

  • Original discount will apply to exchanges
  • If the sale price is no longer available, we'll refund the amount you paid
  • Special promotion discounts may not apply to exchanged items

How do I return a gift?

To return a gift:

  1. The gift giver must initiate the return using their order number
  2. If you don't have the order information, contact us with the gift receipt
  3. Refunds for gifts will be issued to the original payment method
  4. Alternatively, we can issue store credit to the recipient
