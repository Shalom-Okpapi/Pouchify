How to Use Discounts

Applying promo codes and discounts is easy:

Add Items to Your Cart

Browse Pouchify and add products to your cart as usual.

Find the Promo Code Field

At checkout, look for "Promo Code" or "Discount Code" near the payment options.

Enter Your Code

Type your code exactly as shown (case sensitive) and click "Apply."

Complete Your Order

Verify the discount was applied correctly before submitting payment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I combine multiple promo codes?

Pouchify's system only accepts one promo code per order because:

  1. Prevents stacking of incompatible offers
  2. Maintains fair pricing for all customers
  3. Keeps discounts sustainable for artists
  4. Simplifies the checkout process

For maximum savings, look for site-wide sales where discounts are automatically applied.

Do artists earn less when I use a discount?

No, artists receive their full royalty amount when you use:

  • Pouchify-initiated promotions
  • Site-wide sales
  • Official discount codes

The discount comes from Pouchify's margin, not the artist's royalty. Some artist-specific discounts may be funded differently.

How often does Pouchify have sales?

Pouchify typically offers:

  • Major sales around holidays (4-6 times/year)
  • Seasonal promotions quarterly
  • Occasional flash sales (1-2/month)

The best way to catch sales is to subscribe to our newsletter, which often includes exclusive early access.

Can I get a discount after placing my order?

Generally no, but exceptions may include:

  • If the item hasn't shipped yet, you can cancel and reorder
  • For verified website errors displaying incorrect prices

Contact customer support immediately if you believe you qualify for a post-purchase adjustment.
