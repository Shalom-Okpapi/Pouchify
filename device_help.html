Our Model Addition Process

Here's how we evaluate and add new device models:

1. Request Submission

You submit details about your missing model

2. Demand Evaluation

We assess how many requests we get for each model

3. Sourcing

We acquire the device for precise measurements

4. Product Creation

Design templates are created for the new model

5. Quality Testing

Test prints ensure perfect fit before launch

Prioritization Factors

We prioritize models based on:

  • Popularity: Number of requests received
  • Market Share: How widely used the device is
  • Feasibility: Ability to create a quality product
  • Release Date: Newer devices get priority

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to add a new model?

Typical timelines:

  • Popular new phones: 2-4 weeks after release
  • Requested models: 4-8 weeks after reaching threshold
  • Niche devices: May take longer or be grouped with similar sizes

We'll email you when your requested model becomes available.

Can I get notified when my model is added?

Yes! When you submit a request:

  1. We add you to our notification list
  2. You'll receive an email when the model launches
  3. The email includes a special discount for your first order

Make sure to provide an active email address.

What if I need a case urgently?

For immediate needs:

  • Check for universal cases that might fit
  • Look for similar-sized models from the same brand
  • Consider sleeves or pouches instead of form-fitting cases
  • Contact support for personalized recommendations

Do you add older device models?

Our policy on older devices:

  • 0-3 years old: Regular addition process
  • 3-5 years old: Added if we receive enough requests
  • 5+ years old: Rarely added unless exceptional demand

For vintage devices, consider custom orders or universal cases.
