Who We Are

Pouchify provides a premium power bank case that delivers full Protection, Style, & Personalisation!

Founded by Shalom Okpapi, Pouchify was an idea that came out of the disatifaction on how vulnarable power banks were. "Not until Pouchify, power bank cases were either boring, bulky, or lack functionality. I wanted more - I wanted protection, style, and personalisation. But the market offered no solution that combined all three. So I decided to make one for myself, now look at where we are." - Shalom Okpapi.
