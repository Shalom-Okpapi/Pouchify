Account Setup

Follow these steps to create your Pouchify artist account:

Sign Up

Visit Pouchify.com and click "Become an Artist" to create an artist account. Use a valid email address.

Complete Your Profile

Add a profile picture, bio, and links to your social media. This helps customers connect with you.

Set Up Payment

Go to Account Settings ‚Üí Payments to add your PayPal or bank account for royalty payments.

Verify Your Account

Check your email for a verification link. Unverified accounts have limited features.

Profile Best Practices

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for my designs to appear in search?

New designs typically appear in search within:

  • 1-2 hours for basic indexing
  • 24-48 hours for full search visibility
  • Up to 1 week to appear in related product suggestions

Designs with complete metadata (tags, titles, descriptions) rank faster.

Can I sell the same designs on other platforms?

Yes, Pouchify is non-exclusive, meaning you can:

  • Sell the same designs on other print-on-demand sites
  • Offer them as digital downloads elsewhere
  • Sell physical copies yourself

Some artists create platform-exclusive designs as a strategy.

Why was my design removed?

Common reasons for takedowns:

  1. Copyright or trademark infringement
  2. Prohibited content (hate speech, etc.)
  3. Low quality or misaligned artwork
  4. Accidental flagging (can be appealed)

You'll receive an email explaining any removals.

How many designs should I upload to start seeing sales?

While quality matters more than quantity:

  • 10-20 designs - Initial test range
  • 50+ designs - Better discovery chances
  • 100+ designs - Significant portfolio effect

Many successful artists upload 5-10 new designs weekly.
