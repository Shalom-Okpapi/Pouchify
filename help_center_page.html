How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location and the product ordered:

  • Standard shipping: 5-10 business days
  • Express shipping: 2-5 business days
  • International orders: 10-20 business days

You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships. During peak seasons or holidays, delivery times may be longer.

What is your return policy?

We offer returns within 30 days of delivery for most products. To be eligible for a return:

  • Item must be unused and in original condition
  • Item must be in original packaging
  • Proof of purchase is required
How do I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also track your order by:

  1. Logging into your account
  2. Going to "Order History"
  3. Clicking on "Track Package" next to your order

If you're having trouble tracking your package, please contact our support team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Pouchify Gift Cards

We don't currently accept cryptocurrency or bank transfers.

How do I become a seller on Pouchify?

Becoming a seller is easy and free:

  1. Create a free Pouchify account
  2. Go to your Dashboard and click "Add Work"
  3. Upload your original artwork
  4. Set your royalty percentage
  5. Submit your design for review

Once approved, your designs will be available on our products!
