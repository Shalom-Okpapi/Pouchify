Care Instructions

Proper care will keep your Pouchify products looking their best:

  • Power Bank Pouches: Wipe clean with damp cloth
  • Phone Pouches: Wipe clean with damp cloth
  • Stickers: Avoid prolonged moisture

Print Longevity

To maximize the life of printed designs:

  • Wash in cold water only
  • Avoid harsh detergents
  • Don't soak or scrub printed areas
  • Line dry when possible
  • Store properly when not in use

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the product colors shown online?

We strive for color accuracy but several factors can affect what you see:

  • Monitor/screen color calibration varies
  • Different materials absorb ink differently
  • Lighting affects color perception
  • Very bright or neon colors may appear muted

If you receive a product with significantly different colors, contact us about a possible replacement.

Are Pouchify products ethically made?

Pouchify is committed to ethical production:

  • Partner with manufacturers meeting international labor standards
  • Regular factory audits for working conditions
  • Increasing use of organic and sustainable materials
  • Carbon neutral shipping options available
  • Recycled materials used where possible

Why is my design cropped differently on various products?

Design cropping varies because:

  1. Different products have different aspect ratios
  2. Some products require more border space
  3. Print areas vary by product type and size
  4. Artists can customize placement per product

Always check the product preview before purchasing to see how the design will appear.

Can I request a product sample before bulk ordering?

Pouchify doesn't offer product samples, but you can:

  • Order a single item to check quality
  • Review product photos from other customers
  • Check the artist's page for product-specific notes
  • Contact the artist directly with questions
