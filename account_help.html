Login Help

Having trouble accessing your account? Try these solutions:

"Invalid email or password" error

  1. Check for typos in your email and password
  2. Ensure Caps Lock is off
  3. Try copying/pasting your password to avoid mistakes
  4. Use the password reset if needed

Account locked after multiple attempts

For security, accounts are temporarily locked after several failed login attempts. Wait 10 minutes and try again, or reset your password.

Browser Troubleshooting

If login issues persist:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
  • Ensure JavaScript is enabled

Password Reset

Reset your password in just a few steps:

Request Reset Link

Go to the password reset page and enter your email address. You'll receive a code within minutes.

Check Your Email

Look for an email from Pouchify with subject "Password Reset Code" Check spam/junk folders if you don't see it.

Create New Password

Enter the code from the email and input a new password. Choose a strong, unique password you haven't used before.

Login With New Password

Return to Pouchify and login with your email and new password.

Didn't Receive Reset Email?

If you don't get the password reset email:

  1. Wait at least 10 minutes (sometimes delays occur)
  2. Check all email folders including spam/junk
  3. Try submitting the request again
  4. Ensure you're using the email associated with your account
  5. Contact support if you still can't reset

Profile Management

Customize your public profile and account settings:

Update Profile

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Click "Edit Profile"
  3. Upload a profile picture
  4. Write a bio (max 500 characters)
  5. Add links to your website/social media
  6. Save changes

Change Email

  1. Go to Account Settings → Change Email
  2. Enter your new email address
  3. Confirm your current password
  4. Click "Save Changes"
  5. Verify the new email when prompted

Notification Preferences

  • Control email notification frequency
  • Choose which updates you want to receive
  • Enable/disable promotional emails
  • Manage browser notifications

Account Security

Protect your account with these security measures:

✓

Strong Password

Use a unique password with at least 12 characters, including numbers, symbols, and mixed case letters.

✓

Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA in your security settings for an extra layer of protection.

✓

Password Manager

Use a reputable password manager to generate and store strong passwords.

✓

Regular Security Check

Periodically review connected devices and active sessions in your account settings.

Suspicious Activity

If you notice unauthorized changes to your account:

  1. Immediately change your password
  2. Revoke any unfamiliar sessions
  3. Check for unauthorized orders or design changes
  4. Contact support to report the issue

Closing Your Account

To permanently delete your Pouchify account:

Back Up Your Data

Download any designs or information you want to keep. Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.

Resolve Pending Matters

Complete any open orders, withdraw available earnings, and resolve customer service issues.

Submit Deletion Request

Go to Account Settings → Close Account and follow the instructions. You'll need to confirm your password.

Confirmation

You'll receive an email confirming account closure. The process may take up to 10 days to complete.

Alternative to Closing

Consider these options before closing:

  • Deactivate temporarily: Take a break without losing data
  • Remove payment info: Keep account without financial access
  • Delete designs individually: Maintain account but remove specific works

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I merge two Pouchify accounts?

No, Pouchify doesn't currently offer account merging. You'll need to:

  1. Choose which account to keep
  2. Manually transfer designs to that account
  3. Close the secondary account
  4. Update any payment/shipping information

Why can't I change my username?

Pouchify usernames are permanent because:

  • They're tied to your shop URL
  • Changing would break existing links to your work
  • Followers rely on consistent usernames

Choose carefully when creating your account. If absolutely necessary, you'd need to create a new account.

How do I recover an old account?

To recover an old account:

  1. Try logging in with your original email
  2. Use the password reset if needed
  3. Check if the account was automatically closed due to inactivity (after 2+ years)
  4. Contact support with as much original account info as possible

Note: Accounts closed for policy violations cannot be recovered.

Is there an age requirement for Pouchify accounts?

Yes, you must be at least 18 years old to create a Redbubble account. For younger artists:

  • A parent/guardian can create an account to sell the minor's artwork
  • The adult must handle all financial and legal aspects
  • All account communications will go to the adult's email
