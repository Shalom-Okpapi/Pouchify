Payment Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect your payment information. All transactions are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways.

Your payment security is our top priority. We implement several measures to protect your information:

  • Tokenization: Card numbers are replaced with secure tokens
  • 3D Secure: Additional authentication for card payments
  • Fraud monitoring: 24/7 systems to detect suspicious activity
  • No storage: We don't store full credit card numbers on our servers

Secure Checkout Tips

  • Always look for the padlock icon in your browser
  • Verify the URL begins with "https://"
  • Never send payment information via email
  • Use strong, unique passwords for your account

Gift Cards

Redeeming gift cards and store credit is easy:

  1. Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout
  2. Under "Payment Method," select "Gift Card"
  3. Enter your code and click "Apply"
  4. Complete checkout with any remaining balance

Gift Card Terms

  • Gift cards never expire
  • No fees or deductions
  • Can be used for multiple orders until balance is depleted
  • Cannot be redeemed for cash
  • Balance can be checked in your account dashboard

Dynamic Currency Conversion

When paying with credit cards, you may be offered Dynamic Currency Conversion (DCC) by your bank or payment provider. We recommend:

  • Always choose to pay in the merchant's currency (our listed price)
  • DCC rates are often less favorable than your card's rates
  • You may pay additional fees with DCC

Payment Troubleshooting

Having trouble completing your payment? Try these solutions:

Issue Possible Solution
Payment declined Verify card details, ensure sufficient funds, contact your bank
3D Secure authentication failed Check with your bank for verification requirements
Gift card not working Check for typos, ensure card is activated, verify balance
Partial payment methods Combine gift cards with another payment method
Currency mismatch Try changing your store's currency settings
Pending authorization Wait 1-3 business days for hold to release

Still Having Trouble?

If you continue to experience payment issues:

  1. Try a different payment method
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Try a different browser or device
  4. Contact our support team with error details

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to save my credit card information?

Yes, saved payment methods use tokenization for security:

  • We never store your full card number
  • Tokens can only be used on our site
  • You can remove saved methods anytime
  • Additional verification may be required for purchases

Why was my payment method charged twice?

What appears to be a double charge is usually:

  1. Authorization hold: Temporary hold that drops off in 1-3 business days
  2. Separate charges: If you placed multiple orders
  3. Currency conversion: May appear as separate pending transactions

If charges remain after 5 business days, please contact us.

Can I use multiple payment methods for one order?

Yes, you can combine payment methods:

  • Apply a gift card or store credit first
  • Pay remaining balance with another method
  • Maximum of 2 payment methods per order
  • Some restrictions apply to international orders

Do you offer payment plans or installment options?

Yes, we partner with several "Buy Now, Pay Later" services:

  • Klarna: Pay in 4 interest-free installments
  • Afterpay: Split payment into 4 equal parts
  • Affirm: Longer-term financing options

These options appear at checkout if available in your region.
