Pouchify gift cards let you share independent art with friends and family. They're delivered via email and can be used to purchase any product on Pouchify.

Important: Gift cards are non-refundable and can only be used in the currency they were purchased in.

Purchasing Gift Cards

Pouchify offers digital gift cards in several currencies:

Available Currencies

USD, NGN, AUD, EUR, GBP, and CAD

Delivery Method

Email only (no physical cards available)

To purchase a gift card:

  1. Visit the Pouchify gift card page
  2. Select the amount and currency
  3. Enter the recipient's email and a custom message
  4. Complete your payment

Redeeming Gift Cards

Redeeming your gift card is easy:

Add Items to Your Cart

Browse Pouchify and add products to your cart as usual.

Enter Your Gift Card Code

At checkout, look for the "Gift Card" field under payment methods and enter your code.

Complete Your Order

If your gift card covers the full amount, submit your order. For partial payments, enter another payment method for the remaining balance.

Note: Gift cards can't be combined with promo codes in the same order.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions:

Invalid Code

Check for typos and try again.

Missing Email

Check your spam folder. If lost, contact the sender as Pouchify can't resend gift card emails.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a gift card internationally?

No! Gift cards on Pouchify only be used in the currency they were purchased in.

What if my gift card doesn't cover the full order amount?

You'll need to pay the remaining balance with another payment method. Unfortunately, Pouchify doesn't allow splitting payment between multiple gift cards.

Do gift cards expire?

No, Pouchify gift cards don't have an expiration date. However, local laws in some regions may enforce limits on gift card validity.
