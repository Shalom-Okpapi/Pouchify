Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to receive my refund?

Refund processing times:

  • Credit cards: 5-10 business days after processing
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Bank transfer: 7-14 business days

The timing depends on your financial institution. You'll receive an email when the refund is processed.

What if I received a damaged or incorrect item?

For damaged/wrong items:

  1. Submit photos via the return form
  2. We'll email a prepaid return label (where available)
  3. Return the item within 14 days
  4. Receive replacement or refund

In some cases, we may refund without requiring return of the damaged item.

Can I exchange for a different size/color?

Yes, exchanges are available for most items:

  • Select "Exchange" on the return form
  • Specify your preferred replacement
  • Return the original item first
  • New item ships after return is received

If the replacement costs more, you'll pay the difference. If it costs less, you'll receive store credit.

What if my return is past the 30-day window?

Options for late returns:

  • Store credit: May be offered at our discretion
  • Quality issues: We may accept late returns for defects
  • Special circumstances: Contact support for exceptions

Standard refunds cannot be processed after 30 days.
