All orders are subject to product availability. We reserve the right to refuse or cancel any order for any reason, including limitations on quantities available for purchase.

We guarantee the quality of our products. If you receive a defective item, please contact our support team within 30 days of delivery.

Returns & Refunds Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for defective or incorrect items. The item must be unused and in its original packaging.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.
