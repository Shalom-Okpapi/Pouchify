Order Status

Learn how to check your order status, understand tracking information, and what to do if your order is delayed.

How to Check Your Order Status

You can check your order status in several ways:

Online

Log in to your account and visit the Order History page. You'll see the current status of all your recent orders.

Email

Check your email for order confirmation and shipping notifications. Each email includes your current order status.

Mobile App

If you've installed our mobile app, you can check your order status in the "My Orders" section.

What Your Status Means

Here's what each order status means:

Processing

We've received your order and are preparing it for shipment. Most orders process within 1-3 business days.

Shipped

Your order is on its way! Tracking information is now available.

Delivered

Your order has been delivered to the shipping address.

On Hold

There's an issue with your order that needs resolution before we can proceed.

How to Track Your Package

Once your order status changes to "Shipped," you can track your package:

  1. Go to your Order History page
  2. Find the order you want to track
  3. Click "Track Package" next to the order
  4. You'll be redirected to the carrier's tracking page

Tracking Not Immediately Available

Tracking information may take 24-48 hours to appear in the carrier's system after your order ships. If tracking isn't working immediately, please check back later.

Delayed Orders

While we make every effort to deliver orders on time, occasional delays may occur due to:

  • High order volumes during peak seasons
  • Carrier delays
  • Customs clearance for international orders
  • Inclement weather
  • Incorrect shipping address

If your order is significantly delayed:

  1. Check the tracking information for updates
  2. Contact the carrier directly if the package seems stuck
  3. If the carrier can't help, contact our support team

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does order processing usually take?

Most orders process within 1-3 business days. During peak seasons (like holidays), processing may take 3-5 business days.

Why does my order still show "Processing"?

If your order has been in "Processing" for more than 3 business days, it could be because:

  • We're experiencing higher than usual order volume
  • There's an issue with your payment method
  • An item in your order is temporarily out of stock

If you're concerned, please contact support with your order number.

The tracking shows delivered but I didn't receive my package

First, check around your property and with neighbors. Carriers sometimes leave packages in unexpected locations. If you still can't find it:

  1. Contact the carrier to verify the delivery address
  2. File a claim with the carrier if needed
  3. Contact us if the carrier confirms misdelivery

Can I change my shipping address after ordering?

We can only change the shipping address if the order hasn't entered the shipping process. Contact us immediately with your order number and the correct address. Once shipped, address changes must be handled through the carrier.
