Common Solutions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by:

  1. Logging into your Pouchify account
  2. Going to "Order History"
  3. Clicking "Track Package" next to your order

Tracking information is usually available within 3-5 business days after ordering.

Why was my design removed?

Common reasons for design removal include:

  • Copyright or trademark infringement
  • Prohibited content violations
  • Low quality or misaligned artwork

You'll receive an email explaining any removals with instructions for appeal if applicable.
