Shipping Options

We offer several shipping methods to meet your needs:

Standard Shipping $4.99

  • 3-7 business days delivery
  • USPS or FedEx Ground
  • Tracking included
  • Free on orders over $50

Expedited Shipping $12.99

  • 2-3 business days delivery
  • FedEx 2Day or UPS 2nd Day Air
  • Priority processing
  • Free on orders over $100

Overnight Shipping $24.99

  • Next business day delivery
  • FedEx Standard Overnight
  • Order by 12pm ET for same-day processing

Free Shipping Threshold

Enjoy free standard shipping on all orders over $50. Free expedited shipping available on orders over $100. Some exclusions apply for oversized items.

Processing Time

⏱️

All orders typically process within 1-2 business days before shipping. Processing may take longer during:

  • Holiday seasons (November-January)
  • Weekends and public holidays

Weekend Orders

Orders placed after 12pm ET on Friday or on weekends will begin processing the next business day (usually Monday).

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number by email. Here's how to track your package:

  1. Find your tracking number in your shipping confirmation email
  2. Click the tracking link to be taken to the carrier's website
  3. Check for updates - tracking information may take 24 hours to appear

Tracking Not Updating?

If your tracking hasn't updated in more than 48 hours after shipment:

  1. Check the carrier's website directly (not all updates appear in email)
  2. Contact the carrier with your tracking number
  3. If the carrier can't help, contact our support team

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my shipping address after ordering?

We can only change the shipping address if the order hasn't shipped yet. Contact us immediately with your order number and the correct address. Once shipped, address changes must be handled through the carrier.

Why does my tracking show "Label Created" but no movement?

The "Label Created" status means we've prepared your order for shipment but the carrier hasn't scanned it yet. This typically updates within 24 hours of the shipping date. If it doesn't update after 48 hours, please contact us.

Do you ship to PO boxes or APO/FPO addresses?

Yes, we ship to PO boxes via USPS. For APO/FPO addresses:

  • We use USPS Priority Mail Military
  • Delivery times vary (typically 7-14 days)
  • Some product restrictions may apply

What happens if my package is lost in transit?

If your tracking shows no movement for more than 7 business days:

  1. Contact the carrier to open a missing mail search
  2. If the carrier confirms the package is lost, contact us with details
  3. We'll either reship your items or issue a refund
